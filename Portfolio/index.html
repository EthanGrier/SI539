I'm Ethan Grier

User Researcher and UX Designer

My name is Ethan Grier. I am a UX researcher and graphic designer. I am currently a Master's student at the University of Michigan School of Information studying Human Computer Interaction.

My goal is to create designs that evoke positive emotions from the user, are accessible, and easy to understand.

As a psychology major from the University of Michigan, I have extensive experience in the research sector including a co-authorship on a paper presented at the Society for Personality and Social Psychology's annual convention and the creation of a User Experience Tool Kit for the University of Michigan's library.
