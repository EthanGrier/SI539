Here's a Bit About Me...

I grew up in West Bloomfield, MI and spent all the time I had as a child reading, playing with new technology, and studying. I absolutely adored learning and as I got older I only learned to love it more.

I took that passion with me to Kalamazoo College and the University of Michigan where I got my Bachelor's in Psychology and my Bachelor's in Women's Studies. It was there that I realized my love for research. Nothing brought me more joy than designing studies, learning more about the world around us, and compiling research reports.

Currently, I am lucky enough to be getting my Master's of Information in HCI, specializing in User Research and UX Design. When I'm not studying, I can be found at the nature preserve, writing, or playing board games with my friends.
